Increase your account security

Two-step verification, often referred to as Two-factor authentication (2FA), is a security process in which the user provides two authentication factors to verify they are who they say they are, thus providing an additional layer of security. You sign in with something you know (your password) and something you have (a code generated by your phone).

Important tips

-To use two-step verification you must have an specific app on your smartphone such as Google Authenticator, click here for install;

-Before you proceed, configure your smartphone to get date, time and time zone automatically. If you won't do that, it's not gonna work.

How to enable Two-step verification

1. Scan the QR code on your app or enter the key manually;

2. Type the 6-digit code generated by your app on Token box below;

3. Click on Enable 2-Step button.
